Classes for ages 4-6 (90 minute classes, 11-12:30 and 1:00-2:30)

Monday June 3, 2019 - Ooey Gooey Oobleck

Young children will have fun getting messy simulating quicksand and the asthenosphere of the earth with Oobleck. Scientific curiosity starts with and is fed by play, so we will put our curiosity to work experimenting with and playing with slime!

Monday June 17, 2019 - I'm Sensing Science!

Scientists make observations using their five senses. Young kids will love practicing their scientific observation skills by experimenting with their five senses in this class! We will try to identify different objects using only smell, only taste, and only touch. We also have fun experiments with sound and sight.

The cost for 4-6 year old classes is $15/child or $10/child if you register for more than one section.

Classes for ages 6-9 (2 hour 45 minute classes, 1:00-3:45 pm)

Wednesday June 5, 2019 - Blast Off!

We are going to build and launch rockets and do other exciting outer space adventure experiments! This class will absolutely talk about black holes!

Monday June 10, 2019 - Science is Magic! (Repeat from Spring Break)

This class will teach kids TEN different magic tricks that are really just amazing science experiments. Some of these experiments are keeping things dry under water, making water disappear, and making an orb levitate!

Wednesday June 12, 2019 - The Coolest Chemistry

This class has some of my favorite mind-blowing chemistry experiments! We will make color disappear in water, change the density of water to make objects sink or float, and make different shaped crystals.

Wednesday June 19, 2019 - Under Pressure!

These experiments with both air and water pressure are sure to amaze! We will see how temperature affects pressure by compressing a soda can using heat and cold. We will also do awesome experiments with air pressure including stabbing a potato using only a drinking straw and levitating a ping pong ball.

Monday June 24, 2019 - What am I Eating?! (Repeat from Spring Break)

This class is a food-based class, where kids learn what really makes up some of the foods they eat. We will make curds and whey, butter and buttermilk, experiment with how yeast ferments, and do a few fun experiments teaching us how to make scientific observations using our five senses.

Wednesday June 26, 2019 - A Long, Long Time Ago

This class focuses on the ancient formation of rocks and fossils. We will learn about and see different minerals, simulate the formation of each of the different types of rocks in the rock cycle, then kids will be able to make a fossil. Plus kids will get to examine and explore many different rocks and minerals!

The cost for each 6-9 year old class is $25/child or $20/child if you register for more than one section.

Classes for ages 9-12 (2 hour 45 minute classes, 1:00-3:45)

Thursday June 6, 2019 - Solar Power!

We will learn how to harness the power of the electromagnetic spectrum through the sun! We will build and use solar ovens, experimenting to see who can design the most efficient oven. Once we understand how the sun emits UV rays we will experiment with how sunscreen protects us and run an experiment to see how we can best protect ourselves from harmful UV rays.

Thursday June 13, 2019 - Motors, Batteries and Magnets

This exciting class will start with a lemon battery, and help us understand how electricity is transferred. Then we can build our own amazing self-drawing pens! We can also see how to use a battery and pencils to separate water into hydrogen and oxygen gas bubbles (electrolysis). We will also play with some super strong magnets, which is guaranteed fun.

Thursday June 20, 2019 - High Pressure Diving

This class goes even more into depth on how both air and water pressure affect us. Ever wondered how a submarine can sink and rise? In this class we will answer that question by building and experimenting with cartesian divers. We will learn how pressure varies with depth and experiment with both air and water pressure. It turns out that "No Pressure" isn't a comfortable place to be!

Thursday June 27, 2019 - It's pH! Awesome Acids and Bases

Acids and bases are fundamental (and fun!) in Chemistry! We will learn how they work and experiment with some commonly found acids and bases. We will use both a homemade indicator solution and scientific indicator strips to test pH. We will also learn how to do titrations.

The cost for each 9-12 year old class is $30/child or $25/child if you register for more than one section.
