Welcome to Dylan Lyman's Young Scientist Academy, where every child is a scientist!

About The Academy

My Young Scientist Academy is geared toward children ages 6-10. I am a USOE-certified science teacher with 8 years of science teaching experience. My goal is to help kids build confidence by thinking and discovering their way through challenging science projects. We will do experiments, collect data, work as a team, and learn what it means to be a scientist. Each child brings their own "lab coat" (an oversized long-sleeve shirt) and I provide the rest!

Signing Up

Each class is enrolled separately. Your child is welcome to come to any or all of the classes; content will not carry over from class to class. There are 12 (previously 8) spaces per class. If the class you wanted is full, please email me at dylanlyman21@gmail.com and I'll add you to the waitlist; if you don't get in, you'll get priority registration for that class the next time it is offered.

Classes

Each class runs from 1-3:45 pm on the day listed. The content for each class is breifly summarized under the "Classes" tab.

Cost and Payment

Each class costs $20/child, which is all-inclusive. You pay me directly (cash or Venmo: @DylanLyman21).

Safety

All activities at my Young Scientist Academy are age appropriate. I take every necessary step to keep children safe and they will be provided with ample instruction and reminders on how to stay safe. Please emphasize to your child the importance of following directions in order to stay safe and have fun. Students will be required to bring their own "lab coat" (an oversized long-sleeved shirt) that can be ruined. Protective eyewear and gloves will be provided. If you want your child to take any additional safety measures, you may notify me in advance. By signing up to participate, you are agreeing to release me, Dylan Lyman, from any liability in the highly unlikely case of injury.
